GenericBlock(rng::AbstractRNG, N::Int; K0::Int = 1, a0 = 2.0, b0 = 4.0)

Initialize the generic block of a DPM, where N is the sample size, K0 is the initial number of clusters, and (a0, b0) determine the prior distribution of the DP mass parameter, a Gamma(a0, 1 / b0) distribution.

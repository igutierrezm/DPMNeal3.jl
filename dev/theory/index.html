Consider a DPM o in its so-called stick-breaking representation:

\[\begin{aligned} y_i | \bm{d}, \bm{\theta} &\stackrel{\text{\tiny \it ind}}{\sim} q(\cdot | \theta_{d_i}) \\ d_i | \bm{w} &\stackrel{\text{\tiny \it iid}}{\sim} \text{Categorical}(\bm{w}), & \forall i &\in \mathcal{N} \equiv \{1, \ldots, N\} \\ w_j &= v_j \prod\nolimits_{z < j} (1 - v_z), & \forall j &\in \mathbb{N} \\ v_j &\stackrel{\text{\tiny \it iid}}{\sim} \text{Beta}(1, \alpha) \\ \theta_j &\stackrel{\text{\tiny \it iid}}{\sim} g_0(\cdot) \end{aligned}\]
